Work Description

Here, I looked at a variety of string matching techniques and natural language processing (NLP) models for matching job titles and skills. Eventually in my research, I discovered that BERT and FuzzyWuzzy algorithms were the greatest fit for my needs. I ran both the BERT and FuzzyWuzzy algorithms on a set of samples to about 2000 job titles and skills, manually compared the results, and quantified accuracy using a confusion matrix, and the outcomes prompted us to the concluding that the data needed to be cleaned and normalised to improve the algorithm's accuracy.
