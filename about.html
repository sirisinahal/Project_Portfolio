Taxonomy of Building Skills

About:

At first, I thought that creating a Skills Taxonomy was a simple process that could be accomplished simply by organising skills in a hierarchical manner. However, through my follow - up time experience, I've realised the importance of creating a thorough Skills Taxonomy and the time and effort that goes into building it. Scraping data from websites, studying Skills Taxonomies from various websites, normalising and cleaning the data, studying and applying appropriate algorithms to data. To obtain the skills in the required format, working on improving the accuracy of algorithms, and so on are all steps involved in the creation of a Skills Taxonomy. Slight oversight in Skills Substantially reduces the platform's value.
