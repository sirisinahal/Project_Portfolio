Examining the ESCO and NSDC websites:

The National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) was established as part of a national skill development mission to meet India's rising need for trained human resources across industries and close the skills gap.
Its goal is to encourage skill development by stimulating the establishment of big, high-quality, for-profit vocational schools.
There are 24 industry sectors and 36 sector councils that make up the organisation.
ESCO (European Skills, Competences, Qualifications, and Professions) is a thesaurus that characterizes, recognizes, and designates professional occupations and skills that are important to the EU labour market and education and training.
ESCO gives a clear overview in 27 languages of 2942 occupations and 13,485 skills related to these occupations.
